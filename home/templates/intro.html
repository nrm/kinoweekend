KinoWeekend is a non-commercial art festival under time, technical and theme conditions. The main goal is to make up a script according to the given task, then shoot, cut and dub a short film within 72 hours! All the conditions (theme/ object/ film genre) will be announced in the first festival day and the participating works will be assessed by the professional jury and ordinary spectators. Festival participation is FREE OF CHARGE.
