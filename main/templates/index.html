History of the Festival


The first festival KinoWeekend was organized on 8-11th of October 2010, at that time only in St. Petersburg. The idea has been supported by the other “Youth Film Studio”-branches and already in May 2011 the intercity movie-making marathon KinoWeekend took place in 6 cities of Russia at the same time.

  • Dec 10-13, 2010 ̶ St. Petersburg
  • Apr 25-28, 2011 ̶ St. Petersburg
  • May 12-16, 2011 ̶ St. Petersburg, Moscow, Kazan, Nizhny Novgorod, Perm, Yekaterinburg
  • Nov 10-14, 2011 ̶ St. Petersburg
  • Nov 24-28, 2011 ̶ Moscow
  • Dec 1-4, 2011 ̶ Kazan
  • Dec 15-18, 2011 ̶ Nizhny Novgorod
  • Mar 1-5, 2012 – Perm
